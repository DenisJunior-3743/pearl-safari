Who We Are

We are Pearl Safari Uganda

Pearl Safari was founded in Kabale, southwestern Uganda, with a simple but powerful mission — to share the unmatched beauty of Uganda with the world. Nestled between Bwindi Impenetrable Forest and the stunning Lake Bunyonyi, Kabale gave us our deep connection to the natural world.

Over 12 years, we have grown into Uganda's most trusted safari company, guiding hundreds of visitors from every corner of the globe through national parks, mountain forests, ancient lakes, and vibrant cultures. Every journey we plan is crafted with local expertise, genuine care, and an unshakeable commitment to safety.

We believe in responsible tourism that uplifts local communities and protects the ecosystems that make Uganda — the Pearl of Africa — truly extraordinary.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Every decision we make is guided by these four principles.

Conservation First

We operate in harmony with nature, supporting park conservation efforts and funding community-based conservation initiatives through every booking.

Community Benefit

We hire locally, partner with community lodges, and direct a percentage of every tour fee to education and health projects in the communities we visit.

Safety & Trust

All our guides hold national certification. We maintain strict safety protocols for every activity — from gorilla trekking to white-water rafting.

Authentic Experiences

We go beyond the tourist trail. Our guides are storytellers, naturalists, and cultural ambassadors who help you truly connect with Uganda.
